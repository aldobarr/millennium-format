<p><b>Continuous Effects</b> (Japanese: 永続効果 <i>Eizoku Kōka</i>) are a type of monster effect that applies while the monster remains <a href="/wiki/Face-up" title="Face-up">face-up</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Field" title="Field">field</a>. Some of these effects must meet additional requirements before they can be applied, and others may only be applied temporarily. These effects never <a href="/wiki/Activate" title="Activate">activate</a>, and thus, do not start a <a href="/wiki/Chain" title="Chain">Chain</a>.
</p><p>Some Continuous Effects only change the game state under specific temporary situations, rather than continuously changing the game state as long as the monster is face-up on the field. (e.g. "<a href="/wiki/Bountiful_Artemis" title="Bountiful Artemis">Bountiful Artemis</a>") Some Continuous Effects may even have "you can" and/or "<a href="/wiki/Once_per_turn" title="Once per turn">once per turn</a>" clauses, allowing the user to optionally have the Continuous Effect change the game state, without ever activating the effect. (e.g. "<a href="/wiki/Vera_the_Vernusylph_Goddess" title="Vera the Vernusylph Goddess">Vera the Vernusylph Goddess</a>")
</p><p>On cards using <a href="/wiki/Problem-Solving_Card_Text" title="Problem-Solving Card Text">Problem-Solving Card Text</a>, Continuous Effects are easier to recognize due to not using colons or semicolons.
</p><p>In <i><a href="/wiki/Yu-Gi-Oh!_Rush_Duel" title="Yu-Gi-Oh! Rush Duel">Yu-Gi-Oh! Rush Duel</a></i>, Continuous Effects were originally most commonly seen on <a href="/wiki/Maximum_Monster" title="Maximum Monster">Maximum Monsters</a> and <a href="/wiki/Legend_Card" title="Legend Card">Legend Cards</a>. However, as more cards were added, regular <a href="/wiki/Effect_Monster" title="Effect Monster">Effect Monsters</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fusion_Monster" title="Fusion Monster">Fusion Monsters</a> started having them more regularly. Some themes such as <a href="/wiki/Ancient_Gear_(Rush_Duel_series)" title="Ancient Gear (Rush Duel series)"> Ancient Gear</a> and <a href="/wiki/Evasive_Chaos_Ninsect" title="Evasive Chaos Ninsect">Evasive Chaos Ninsect</a> are revolved around having Continuous Effects and support monsters with Continuous Effects of their respective <a href="/wiki/Type" title="Type">Type</a> and/or <a href="/wiki/Attribute" title="Attribute">Attribute</a>.  They are denoted by the text <b>[Continuous Effect]</b> in <a href="/wiki/Card_text" title="Card text">card text</a>.
</p>
